Shock / Vibration

Peak impact energy per window — red line is the alert threshold
A sudden shock (bearing slip, impact, imbalance) shows up as a sharp spike above the threshold. The sensor is checked continuously; a spike that crosses the line for even a moment counts as a shock event.

Temperature

Bearing / motor temperature — red line is the alert threshold
Temperature rises slowly, so it catches gradual problems like friction or lubrication loss — a different failure mode than a sudden shock.
